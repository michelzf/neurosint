## English

**Neurosint is NOT a medical device and NOT medical advice.**

- **Not a medical device.** Not cleared by the FDA, ANVISA, or any regulator. Not "Software as a
  Medical Device" (SaMD).
- **Does not diagnose, prescribe, or treat.** It is an **information-organization and
  reasoning-support tool** for educational and personal use. Every clinical decision belongs to
  the **responsible healthcare professional**.
- **AI can make mistakes and may occasionally hallucinate.** It isn't the expected behavior, but
  language models sometimes invent a fact, a value or a reference — and once is enough to mislead.
  That's why Neurosint is a **copilot**, not a replacement: **cross-check against the primary
  source** (the report, the physician, the literature) before acting. This project's own exam
  parser has produced incorrect values on some reports — it's worth verifying.
- **Validate everything with professionals.** Nothing here replaces a consultation or medical follow-up.
- **In an emergency, seek medical care immediately.**

### Privacy & health data

- The data you enter is **sensitive health data of a loved one.** Handle it with care.
- **Cloud APIs send that data to third parties**, who may log it per their policies. See
  `docs/PRIVACIDADE.md` and consider the local mode.
- **Compliance with GDPR / local data-protection law is the user's responsibility.**
- Always work in a **private** repository when using real data.
